Search and Answer

Retrieves, ranks, and cites web sources in real time to produce grounded answers

Real World Analogy

Like a research librarian who does not answer from memory. When you ask a question, the librarian walks into the stacks, pulls the most relevant books, cross references them, and writes you a summary with page numbers so you can verify every claim.

How It Works

Search and answer harnesses power AI search engines like Perplexity. When you ask a question, the system crawls the web in real time, retrieves relevant pages, ranks them for quality, and feeds the best sources to the AI. The AI then writes a clear answer with citations pointing back to where the information came from.

The heavy lifting happens before the AI ever sees anything. Perplexity, for example, maintains an index of over 200 billion URLs and uses multiple retrieval methods simultaneously to find the most relevant content. Complex questions get broken into sub questions that are searched in parallel, and the results are cross referenced before being assembled into a final answer.

What makes these systems different from a regular chatbot is the emphasis on factual accuracy and freshness. Every claim should trace back to a source. The AI is not relying on what it learned during training but on what it just found on the web.

Components

  • Real time web crawling and indexing at massive scale
  • Query decomposition that breaks complex questions into parallel searches
  • Hybrid retrieval combining keyword matching with semantic understanding
  • Source ranking and deduplication before passing to the AI
  • Inline citation generation tied to specific source passages
  • Proprietary inference engines optimized for search workloads

Key Terms

RAG

Retrieval Augmented Generation. A technique where AI retrieves relevant documents before generating an answer, grounding its response in actual sources.

Hybrid search

Combining traditional keyword matching with AI powered semantic understanding to find results that match both exact terms and overall meaning.

Grounding

Connecting AI generated claims to verifiable sources, reducing the chance of AI making things up.
