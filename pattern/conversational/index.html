Conversational

Manages multi turn dialogue, memory, persona, and user interaction

Real World Analogy

Like a skilled concierge at a luxury hotel. The concierge remembers your name, your preferences from past visits, and adapts their tone to each guest. The concierge is not the hotel itself, just the person making the experience feel personal and continuous.

How It Works

When you chat with an AI assistant like ChatGPT or Claude, the software around the AI manages your entire conversation. It remembers what you said earlier in the chat, keeps track of your preferences across sessions, and maintains a consistent personality. Without this layer, the AI would treat every message as if it came from a stranger with no history.

The conversation harness handles everything from storing your chat history to deciding which AI model should respond. Some systems keep detailed memories of past interactions so the assistant feels personalized over time. Customer support versions add specialized features like pulling answers from help articles, detecting when to hand off to a human agent, and tracking whether your issue was actually resolved.

The key challenge is making conversations feel continuous and personal despite the AI having a limited window of text it can process at once. The harness uses techniques like summarizing older messages, storing important facts separately, and strategically injecting relevant memories into each new exchange.

Architecture

Components

  • Conversation history management and summarization
  • Persistent memory across sessions
  • Persona and identity layer with behavioral constraints
  • Multi model routing for selecting the best AI per task
  • Tool integration for code execution, web browsing, and file analysis
  • Projects and workspaces with scoped instructions

Key Terms

Context window

The maximum amount of text an AI can consider at once. Older messages must be trimmed or summarized to stay within this limit.

System prompt

Hidden instructions given to the AI before your conversation starts, defining its personality, rules, and behavior.

Memory persistence

The ability for an AI to remember facts about you across separate conversations.
